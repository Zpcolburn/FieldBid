# 🛠️ FieldBid - Simple Contractor Job Manager

A simple, mobile-first app for small contractors to manage estimates, client signatures, job scheduling, job notes, receipts, invoices, payments, and recurring work.

FieldBid helps contractors move from estimate to paid invoice without complicated software.

No bloated dashboards. No confusing setup. Just the tools small contractors actually need to run jobs from the field.

## ✨ Features

### 📋 Estimate Management

- Create professional estimates
- Add customer information
- Add job address
- Add scope of work
- Add labor, material, equipment, and miscellaneous line items
- Upload job photos to estimates
- Add terms, exclusions, and expiration dates
- Set deposits and estimated start dates
- Send estimates by email or shareable link
- Allow clients to approve and sign estimates online

---

### ✍️ Client Approval & Signatures

- Clients can view estimates from a secure link
- Clients can review scope, photos, pricing, and terms
- Clients can approve and electronically sign
- Estimate status updates automatically after approval
- Approved estimates can become scheduled jobs

---

### 📅 Job Scheduling

- Convert approved estimates into jobs
- Schedule job date and time
- Track job status
- View upcoming jobs
- Get reminders before scheduled work
- Review job scope before arriving on site
- See likely needed materials, equipment, and notes

---

### 📝 Job Notes & Client Updates

- Add daily notes
- Add weekly updates
- Upload job progress photos
- Track delays, weather notes, completed work, and next steps
- Send clean updates to clients
- Keep all job communication organized in one place

---

### 🧾 Receipt & Expense Tracking

- Upload receipt photos to a job
- Save vendor, date, amount, category, and notes
- Organize expenses by job, month, year, and category
- Export expenses for a bookkeeper or tax professional
- Keep receipts tied directly to the work they belong to

---

### 💳 Invoices & Payments

- Convert estimates into invoices
- Adjust final invoice amounts
- Add extra charges or change items
- Send invoice links to clients
- Track invoice status
- Accept online payments through Stripe
- Mark cash or check payments manually

---

### 🔁 Recurring Invoices

- Create recurring invoices for repeat work
- Useful for mowing, maintenance, cleaning, property services, and service contracts
- Support weekly, biweekly, monthly, quarterly, or custom schedules
- Automatically send recurring invoices in future versions

---

### 🤖 AI-Assisted Features

Future AI tools may include:

- Clean up rough job notes
- Turn notes into professional client updates
- Suggest missing estimate line items
- Create job preparation checklists
- Summarize scope of work
- Categorize receipts
- Extract receipt details from photos
- Generate invoice notes
- Help contractors communicate clearly without extra paperwork
